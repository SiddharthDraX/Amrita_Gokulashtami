Gokulashtami

Celebrations at Amrita's Coimbatore Campus

Reminding of the goal of making oneself and others happy, Gokulashtami was celebrated at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Coimbatore campus, with great fervor, enthusiasm and fanfare. On birthday of Sri Krishna, Chancellor Sri Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma) reminds us, “We can transform our lives into an experience of spreading happiness to one and all. Sri Krishna's life was the perfect example of this. If all our infinite, innate auspicious qualities were to take the form of a human being, the beautiful result would be Lord Krishna. He expressed the infinite power inherent in humankind to its fullness.”

Rituals peformed by Dignataries.

Sahasranama Archana followed by Nagara Sankeerthana wherein a group of students and faculty across campus singing Lord Krishna's glory.

Marching of Floats.

Richly decorated floats, grandly Marching, depicting various stages from the life of Sri Krishna. Accompanied by girls and boys dressed as Krishnas and Gopikas, the floats goes around the campus, stopping at various points, where students sang and danced.

Prasadam disribution.

As always Swamiji distribute “Prasadam” for entire Amritians.

Finals of Cultural Competion.

students participated in various competitions like dance, music, skit, quiz, elocution, painting and Rangoli, all on the theme of Sri Krishna.

Sports activities.

Prize Distribution of culturals and sports.
